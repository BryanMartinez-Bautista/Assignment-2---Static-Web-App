About This Gallery

Welcome to the Batman Universe Character Gallery

The Batman Universe Character Gallery showcases some of the most iconic heroes and villains from the Batman Universe and DC Comics.

Why I Chose This Theme

I chose the Batman Universe as my theme because I'm fascinated by how Batman fights crime without having any superpowers. Not only does he fight crime without powers, but he is also considered one of the world's greatest detectives. He solves some of the most difficult cases in Gotham City, ranging from homicides with no evidence to investigations that seem to have reached a dead end. Batman is also a humble person who doesn't let his wealth get the best of him. Instead, he uses his money to help others and to develop the advanced gadgets and technology he uses to fight crime. One thing I admire most about Batman is the code he follows. No matter how terrible a criminal's actions are, Batman refuses to kill them because he believes justice should be served without taking a life. As for the design of my website, I chose colors like black, blue, and yellow because they represent the traditional Batman theme. I also decided to use cards for each character because they organize the information in a clean and easy-to-read layout. I wanted the character cards to stand out and almost feel like they were jumping off the screen while keeping the overall design simple and visually appealing.
