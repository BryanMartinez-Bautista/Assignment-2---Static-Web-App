Quick Info

Batman

The Dark Knight of Gotham City, a vigilante hero who fights crime using advanced gadgets and detective skills.

Role:
Vigilante Hero

First Comic Appearance:
Detective Comics #27 (May 1939)

Backstory

Batman is a vigilante hero who fights crime in Gotham City. Behind the mask is Bruce Wayne, a wealthy businessman and the owner of Wayne Enterprises. Bruce became Batman after both of his parents were killed in an alley outside a theater when he was a child. Since then, he has dedicated his life to protecting Gotham City and stopping criminals. Using his wealth, intelligence, and advanced gadgets from Wayne Enterprises, Batman fights crime without having any superpowers.

Abilities

  • Master martial artist trained in multiple combat styles.
  • Expert detective with exceptional intelligence.
  • Uses advanced gadgets, vehicles, and technology to fight crime.
